## Overview

Diagnosed slowdown in new advertising revenue growth by analyzing product signals and survey data from 5,000+ advertisers. Identified churn behaviour in novice users and led 8+ cross-functional teams to redesign onboarding with AI-powered support and routing.

## Problem

New advertising revenue growth was experiencing a significant slowdown. Analysis revealed that novice users were churning at critical points in the onboarding journey, impacting overall business metrics.

## Solution

Led a comprehensive redesign of the onboarding experience by:

- Analyzing product signals and survey data from 5,000+ advertisers to identify friction points
- Diagnosing specific churn behaviors in novice user segments
- Coordinating with 8+ cross-functional teams to implement AI-powered support and intelligent routing
- Creating data-driven recommendations for onboarding flow optimization

## Key Initiatives

- Data Analysis: Deep-dive into product signals and survey data to uncover root causes of churn
- AI Integration: Implemented AI-powered support system to guide users through onboarding
- Smart Routing: Developed intelligent routing to match users with appropriate resources
- Cross-functional Leadership: Aligned engineering, product, marketing, and customer success teams

## Results

- **1.5× QoQ** new business revenue growth
- **3.0× increase** in onboarding completion rates
- **+10% CSAT** improvement in customer satisfaction scores
- Successfully reduced novice user churn through targeted interventions

## Impact

This initiative demonstrated the power of combining data analysis, AI technology, and cross-functional collaboration to drive meaningful business outcomes and improve user experience at scale.
