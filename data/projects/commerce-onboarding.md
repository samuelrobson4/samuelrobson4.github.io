## Overview

Conducted competitor benchmarking and interviews with 2,000+ merchants to reimagine the commerce onboarding experience. Introduced streamlined verification and gamification strategies, with roadmap recommendations adopted by product VPs.

## Problem

The existing commerce onboarding process created friction for new merchants, leading to drop-offs and delays in time-to-first-sale. The experience needed to be reimagined to meet modern merchant expectations and competitive standards.

## Research Approach

Comprehensive research methodology combining multiple data sources:

- **Competitor Benchmarking**: Analyzed onboarding flows from leading commerce platforms
- **User Interviews**: Conducted in-depth interviews with 2,000+ merchants to understand pain points
- **Journey Mapping**: Documented current-state experience and identified friction points
- **Opportunity Analysis**: Synthesized findings into actionable recommendations

## Key Recommendations

- **Streamlined Verification**: Simplified identity and business verification process to reduce friction
- **Gamification Elements**: Introduced progress indicators and milestone celebrations to increase engagement
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Redesigned information architecture to reduce cognitive load
- **Contextual Guidance**: Added just-in-time help and support throughout the journey

## Results & Impact

- Roadmap recommendations **adopted by product VPs** and integrated into product strategy
- Research insights informed multi-quarter product roadmap
- Created foundation for improved merchant experience and reduced time-to-value
- Established framework for ongoing merchant research and iteration

## Stakeholder Engagement

Successfully influenced product direction through:

- Executive presentations to VP-level stakeholders
- Cross-functional workshops with product, design, and engineering teams
- Creation of actionable, prioritized roadmap recommendations
- Data-driven storytelling to build consensus around proposed changes
